About NotesFriend

NotesFriend is a dedicated educational platform created to support students studying under NMU University syllabus. Our goal is to provide easy access to important questions, notes, video lectures, and classroom links — all in one place.

We understand that students often struggle to find structured study material. That's why NotesFriend organizes subject-wise resources semester by semester, helping students prepare efficiently for exams.

This platform is built with passion to empower learners and make education more accessible, simple, and modern.
